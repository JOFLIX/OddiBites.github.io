About Us

OddiBites is a street food vendor dedicated to serving high-quality grilled and fried chicken and eggs. We use fresh ingredients and our secret recipes to create mouthwatering dishes that will leave you craving for more. Our mission is to provide delicious food with excellent service, making every meal a memorable experience.

Contact

Have questions or want to place an order? Contact us via WhatsApp or visit us at our location on Main Street. We accept Mpesa and cash payments. We're here to serve you!
